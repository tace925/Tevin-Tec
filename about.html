The story behind Tevin's Tech

how it started

In 2022, Tevin Mulinge was a computer science student with a simple idea: build beautiful, functional websites for entrepreneurs who needed an online presence. What started as small portfolio projects quickly grew into something bigger.

Clients loved the work. They referred friends. Soon, Tevin couldn't handle all the requests alone. That's when the idea of building a team came to life.

Today, Tevin's Tech is a collective of skilled programmers who share the same vision: build systems that actually work, help businesses grow, and make technology accessible to everyone.
