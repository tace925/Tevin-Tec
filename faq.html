frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

How much does a website cost?

+

Pricing depends on complexity. Personal portfolios start from KES 45,000, business websites from KES 80,000, and custom systems are quoted based on requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and quote.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Simple portfolios take 1-2 weeks. Business websites take 2-4 weeks. Complex systems take 4-8 weeks depending on requirements. We'll provide a timeline in your quote.

Do I need to provide content?

+

Yes, you'll need to provide text, images, and branding materials. If you need help with content, we can recommend copywriters and photographers.

What is dark mode and why should I have it?

+

Dark mode is a color scheme that uses light text on dark backgrounds. It reduces eye strain, saves battery on mobile devices, and is a modern feature users expect. All our sites include dark mode.

Will my website work on phones?

+

Absolutely! We build all sites with mobile-first design, ensuring they look perfect on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Do you offer ongoing support?

+

Yes! We offer maintenance packages starting from KES 5,000/month that include security updates, backups, and content changes.

Do I own my website?

+

Yes, you own everything. We transfer all files and code to you upon completion. You're free to take it elsewhere or have us continue maintaining it.

How do I start?

+

Simply contact us for a free consultation. We'll discuss your needs, provide a quote, and get started!
